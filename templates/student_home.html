🏪 About LabHatud

LabHatud is Cantilan's premier smart laundry ecosystem, founded with a mission to make laundry day effortless for everyone.

Our Vision
To revolutionize the laundry industry in the Philippines through technology, creating seamless connections between customers, laundry shops, and delivery riders.

Our Values

  • 🤝 Reliability: We deliver on our promises.
  • 🌱 Sustainability: Eco-friendly practices in every partnership.
  • 💡 Innovation: Continuous improvement through technology.
  • ❤️ Community: Supporting local businesses and riders.

Founded: 2024

Headquarters: Cantilan, Surigao del Sur, Philippines
